Some other interests of mine include swimming, national parks, and Sichuan cuisine. I also enjoy classical music—in particular, virtuosic pieces such as Conus's Violin Concerto in E minor and Vieuxtemps's Violin Concerto No. 5, both performed by Jascha Heifetz.
